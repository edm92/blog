Who is He?

Currently Making Something People Want.

Evan Morrison holds a BS in Computer Science from the University of Wollongong with majors in Secure Distributed Systems and Software Development along with a Ph.D. in Computer Science where his area of research was artificial intelligence applied to process management. 

He has given presentations around the country and internationally in New Zealand, South East Asia, and Europe. Evan even has prepared a patent application based on search technologies. He is a regular at technology centric meetup groups across Sydney. 

Evan is has a long list of directorships and past accomplishments. He has been:

  • managing director for eHealth consulting company Progression Logic Pty Ltd who develop business intelligence systems, 

  • the principle of Expert Decisions which provides research and innovation-based services in various industry sectors including logistics, finance, and media. 

    • Growth of business from startup to profit generation and has employed roughly 10 people through its history

    • Assisted achieve over $1.5m in research funding

  • managing director for modern financial services consultancy AUGY Pty Ltd
